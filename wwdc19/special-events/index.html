Special Events

Have fun, unwind, and make valuable connections with fellow attendees at exciting events throughout the week.

Apple Design Awards

Join us as the winners are revealed. Learn about the remarkable developers who used their ingenuity, smarts, and savvy to build the year’s best apps for Apple platforms.

  • McEnery Convention Center
  • Monday: 5:00 – 6:00 PM

Morning HIIT Workout with Aaptiv

Start your day with the fantastic trainers of Aaptiv, the number one audio fitness app on the App Store. This 40-minute high intensity interval training workout will wake you up and push you to your limits leaving you energized and ready to take on the day. All levels are welcome. Check-in starts at 6:30 a.m.

  • Activity Studio
  • Tuesday: 7:00 – 8:00 AM

Registration Closed

WWDC Run with Joan Benoit Samuelson

Celebrate Global Running Day with an invigorating run through San Jose led by the amazing Joan Benoit Samuelson, winner of the Boston Marathon, the Chicago Marathon, and the first ever Women’s Olympic Marathon. All levels are invited to join this out-and-back 5K run. Check-in starts at 6:30 a.m.

  • Activity Studio
  • Wednesday: 7:00 – 8:00 AM

Registration Closed

Yoga Mind-Body Reboot with Glo

Make space in your week to reflect and recharge with instructors from the wildly popular Glo — Yoga and Meditation app. This gentle flow practice combines mindfulness and movement to leave you feeling fully reset and inspired. All levels are welcome. Check-in starts at 6:30 a.m.

  • Activity Studio
  • Thursday: 7:00 – 8:00 AM

Registration Closed

Music on the Plaza

Enjoy live music, a selection of local beers, and the company of fellow developers. Attendees 21 years and older may drink alcohol and can pick up a wristband for drinks on Tuesday on the plaza outside McEnery Convention Center by showing their valid government-issued photo ID.

Bash

Experience incredible music, food, and drinks at Discovery Meadow, just steps away from the McEnery Convention Center. Attendees 21 years and older may drink alcohol and can pick up a wristband for drinks on Thursday either on the plaza outside McEnery Convention Center or at the tent inside Discovery Meadow by showing their valid government-issued photo ID. Bag check is not available at the bash, so please leave your bags at the hotel.

  • Discovery Meadow
  • Thursday: 7:00 – 10:00 PM
